Household details for Kenyans

Jambo Wakenya! I hope you are all saying safe. During this pandemic, the government at large would like to be involved in supporting the basic affairs of Kenyans during this hard time.

The head of the family is obliged to input the details of every member of the household so as to enhance accurate information.

He/she is required to send out the copies of every particular member to the area representative in due time.


















Stay safe!
